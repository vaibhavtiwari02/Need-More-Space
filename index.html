OUR OBJECTIVE

Several spacecraft, including the Parker Solar Probe, are gathering data about the Sun that will enable us to learn more about solar effects (a.k.a. space weather) in space and on Earth. Our objective is to create an interactive website to represent this data in a new and inviting way that improves public knowledge and understanding of space weather.
